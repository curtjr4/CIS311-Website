Overview

I am a part-time college student pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems with a Cybersecurity emphasis while also working as a Desktop Support Technician and helping my company's IT Infrastructure and Operations team. Ever since I was a little kid, I always enjoyed tinkering around with technology -- figuring out how a device works, how to create computer programs to make life easier, and building new computers. I aspire to move into a full-time Linux System Administrator role once I am done with my bachelor's degree and earn my Red Hat Certified System Administrator certificate, as well as my Cisco CCNA certificate.

Academic Asperations

I am currently a senior at Colorado State University at Pueblo, and currently hold the following credentials:

  • Associates of Applied Science in Cybersecurity, issued by Arapahoe Community College in Littleton, CO.
  • Certificate of Computer Information Systems, issued by Arapahoe Community College in Littleton, CO.
  • Linux Pro, issued by TestOut Corporation.
  • Guardicore Certified Segmentation Administrator, issued by Akamai Technologies.
  • National Cyber League Spring 2021 Individual Game, issued by Cyber Skyline. This is a capture the flag event for computer hacking skills, and I ranked in the 97th percentile overall, and 77th percentile in the gold bracket.

Work History

Since I have been out of high school, I have primarily worked in customer service with King Soopers (owned by Kroger). The experience that I have had working with customers face-to-face has proven to be invaluable as I have been working as a Desktop Support Technician with Land Title Guarantee Company, and will continue to help me as I continue to learn and grow in my career.
